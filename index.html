Rules

  • There are 2 white balls, 1 red ball, and 1 green ball
  • If you fail to return a white ball, the other player gets 1 point
  • Letting the green ball pass gives you a paddle buff
  • Hitting the red ball will shorten your paddle
  • Whoever hits 20 points first wins!
